Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You will need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name, and a form of identification like a passport or national ID card. The specific requirements may vary slightly depending on the location and vehicle type.

Is insurance included in the rental price?

Basic insurance is typically included, but we offer several additional coverage options for your peace of mind. You can review all insurance options during the booking process or ask our staff for details.

Can I have the car delivered to my location?

Yes, we offer a delivery and collection service to airports, hotels, or specific addresses within our service area. Additional fees may apply. Please specify your delivery needs when making a reservation.

What is you cancellation policy?

You can cancel for free up to 48 hours before your scheduled pick-up time. Cancellations made within 48 hours may be subject to a fee. Please refer to our terms and conditions for full details.
